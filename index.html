Our Story

More than CeMAP training

Rockgate Academy was created from lived experience and from seeing how confusing the journey into mortgage advice can feel for people starting from scratch. Too often, learners are shown how to pass exams, but not how to understand the bigger picture.

For many people, CeMAP is only the beginning. The real challenge is understanding what comes next — the pathways, the choices, the confidence, and the direction needed to move from qualification into a real career. Rockgate Academy was built to make that journey clearer, more practical, and more supportive.

Our Mission

To help learners build clarity, confidence, and career direction

We do not just want learners to memorise information. We want them to understand the industry, believe in their ability to succeed, and move forward with a stronger sense of direction.

Meet the Founder

A more personal and practical approach to learning

Rockgate Academy was built from a genuine desire to help people navigate a journey that can often feel unclear at the start.

After CeMAP, I experienced something many learners do not always talk about openly — uncertainty about what came next. Passing exams was an important milestone, but it did not automatically bring clarity about the next step, the wider industry pathway, or how to move forward with confidence.

That experience, together with seeing how many aspiring professionals are left with similar questions, played a big part in the creation of Rockgate Academy and the desire to offer a more complete and supportive experience.

Frequently Asked Questions

Questions learners often ask

Is Rockgate Academy only for people already in financial services?

No. Rockgate Academy is designed to support beginners, career changers, and learners who want a clearer route into the mortgage industry.

No. CeMAP is the starting point, but Rockgate Academy also helps learners understand what comes after qualification and how to think about their pathway into the industry.

Is this suitable if I feel overwhelmed or unsure where to begin?

Yes. The whole approach is designed to make the journey clearer, simpler, and more supportive for people who need structure and confidence.
